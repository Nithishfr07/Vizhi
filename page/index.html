About Us

Vizhi is a social initiative aimed at making sustainability a convenient lifestyle and household practice. We represent a proactive response to the climate crisis by harnessing the power of technology and community engagement to create a resource hub for all. Vizhi will serve as a one-stop solution for any climate-related queries, providing users with tailored resources and information on sustainable living.

Recycle Map Locator

The recycler locator is a tool that helps you effortlessly identify the nearest recycling units. Additionally, by supporting you with relevant contact details, it helps you dispose of recyclable materials responsibly at your convenience.

  • Promotes proper waste disposal, ensuring that recyclable materials are directed to appropriate facilities rather than ending up in landfills, thereby reducing the overall burden on waste management systems.
  • Encourages the adoption of a circular waste management policy, fostering a more sustainable approach to resource utilization.
  • Makes recycling facilities easily accessible, and thereby facilitates increased participation in recycling initiatives.

Sustainable Shopping Guide

The Sustainable Shopping Guide is a feature designed to empower consumers with the knowledge to make eco-friendly choices. By aiding you to identify the most sustainably sourced products available in nearby stores, this guide serves as a catalyst for informed and conscientious shopping practices.

  • Facilitates access to information on sustainable options, and fosters a broader awareness of eco-friendly products available nearby.
  • Encourages the purchase of products with minimal environmental impact, and empowers consumers to make choices aligned with their values.
  • Promotes sustainable and responsible sourcing practices by businesses and supports companies that prioritize environmentally friendly production methods.

About Blog

The Processes & Policies blog helps readers to be informed about the latest developments in sustainable living. The blog showcases existing and new sustainable alternatives for everyday processes and, at the same time, highlights important sustainability policies. By grounding policy discussions in real-world examples and practical considerations, the blog breaks down complex issues into pieces of easy-to-understand blog.

  • Empowers readers to adopt environmentally conscious habits in their daily routines and also brings to their attention policies affecting the environment.
  • Creates a bridge between policy discussions and practical applications and reinforces the idea that sustainability requires both individual and systemic changes.
  • Serves as a centralized hub for readers looking to deepen their understanding about sustainability, by offering insights about micro-level processes and macro-level policies alike.

Climate Solutions Marketplace

Specifically designed for users who love innovations, be it cutting-edge technology or ingenious process breakthroughs, the Climate Solutions Marketplace is a collection of offerings from companies that are building inventive solutions dedicated to sustainability. It directs attention to companies at the forefront of the sustainability movement, showcasing their innovative climate solutions you can buy or adopt.

  • Spotlights companies that are pioneers in developing and implementing innovative climate solutions, providing a stage for their groundbreaking work.
  • Encourages a broader appreciation for technologies and process breakthroughs dedicated to further sustainability.
  • Promotes and appreciates the integration of these eco-friendly technologies and process innovations into everyday life.
